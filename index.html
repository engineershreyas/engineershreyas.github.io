Professional Experience

My professional experience is a culmination of internships and freelance projects I have done over the years. Most recently, I completed a software engineering internship at Amazon, where I built a back-end service using AWS and the Java Spring framework. I also completed a Mobile Engineering internship for Canary, where I used Bluetooth Low Energy technologies to implement a solution that improved upon a previous feature of the product's Android application. I am also the CTO/Co-Founder of MySwapp, a mobile application that implements a modern form of bartering by allowing users to trade old items they no longer want with other users in their local area. Previous experience includes being a Computer Science Research Assistant in the Aresty Summer Science program at Rutgers where I built this. Also, I am one of the Android developers for the Rutgers 250th anniversary application.
